# WYZE Cam Pan v3 Review: "Panoramic Peace of Mind"

## Introduction:
The **WYZE Cam Pan v3** is a versatile and affordable security camera that offers comprehensive surveillance both indoors and outdoors. Its pan-and-tilt capability, combined with an IP65 weather-resistant rating, makes it a robust choice for monitoring your home, pets, or loved ones.

## Overview
This smart home security camera boasts **1080p HD video quality**, **360° panning**, and **180° tilting** to ensure every corner is covered. With **color night vision** and **24/7 continuous recording**, it promises clear footage around the clock.

## Pros and Cons:

__Pros__:
- **Affordable price** at just $39.0.
- **Indoor/Outdoor flexibility** with an IP65 rating.
- **Full HD 1080p** video with **color night vision**.
- **Motion tracking** keeps tabs on movement seamlessly.
- **Two-way audio** and compatibility with **Alexa & Google Assistant**.

__Cons__:
- Requires **Wyze Outdoor Power Adapter v2** for outdoor use (sold separately).
- **5 GHz Wi-Fi not supported**; only 2.4 GHz.
- Some users reported **connection issues** with the right-angle micro USB connector.

### Design and Build Quality:
The WYZE Cam Pan v3's design is sleek and modern, fitting unobtrusively in any setting. Its build is solid, designed to withstand diverse weather conditions, making it a reliable choice for both indoor and outdoor use.

### Performance and Functionality:
Users report that the camera performs well, with **fast and smooth operation**. The **rotating motion-tracking** feature is a highlight, accurately following movement and providing comprehensive coverage.

### User Experience:
Setup is straightforward, with the **Wyze app** guiding users through the process. The camera's operation is quiet and unobtrusive, and the app offers a wealth of customizable options.

### Value for Money:
At its price point, the WYZE Cam Pan v3 offers an impressive array of features that are typically found in more expensive models, making it an excellent value for money.

### Verdict:
The WYZE Cam Pan v3 stands out as a **cost-effective, feature-rich** security camera. It's a solid choice for anyone looking to enhance their home security without breaking the bank.

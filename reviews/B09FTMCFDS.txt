# SteelSeries Apex 7 TKL Compact Mechanical Gaming Keyboard Review: Illuminate Your Play

## Introduction:
The SteelSeries Apex 7 TKL is a compact mechanical gaming keyboard that promises to bring both style and substance to your gaming setup. With its **OLED Smart Display** and **RGB Backlit** features, it's designed to keep you informed and in control without missing a beat.

## Overview
Priced at **$118.00**, the Apex 7 TKL boasts **durable mechanical gaming switches** rated for **50 million keypresses** and offers a choice between red, blue, or brown switches to suit your typing preference. The **Aircraft Grade Aluminum Alloy Frame** ensures longevity, while the **PrismCaps** and **Dynamic Per Key RGB Illumination** allow for extensive customization.

## Pros and Cons:

__Pros__:
- **OLED Smart Display** keeps important settings visible.
- **PrismCaps** enhance the RGB lighting effect.
- **Durable construction** with the Series 5000 metal frame.
- **Customizable RGB illumination** for a personalized setup.

__Cons__:
- Some users reported **reliability issues** with the LED and electronics.
- The **non-detachable cable** and requirement for **two USB ports** may inconvenience some users.
- A **full keyboard version** is desired by some for a complete experience.

### Design and Build Quality:
The Apex 7 TKL features a sleek design with a robust aluminum frame. Its **compact size** is perfect for setups with limited space, and the **quality of materials** used suggests a product built to last.

### Performance and Functionality:
The keyboard performs admirably in gaming scenarios, with **fast actuation** and **quiet switches** that don't sacrifice responsiveness. The **OLED display** and **media controls** add a level of convenience, though some users have noted the **OLED's brightness** could be improved.

### User Experience:
Setting up the Apex 7 TKL is straightforward, and the **SteelSeries Engine software** provides ample customization options. However, the **software's user-friendliness** could be enhanced, as per some user feedback.

### Value for Money:
At its price point, the Apex 7 TKL offers a good balance between **features and quality**. It stands competitively against other gaming keyboards in its category, especially for those who prioritize **aesthetics and build quality**.

### Verdict:
The SteelSeries Apex 7 TKL is a solid choice for gamers who want a **reliable, customizable, and visually appealing** keyboard. While it has its drawbacks, its strengths make it a worthy consideration.
